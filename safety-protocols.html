Safety Protocols

Due to the outbreak of the 2019 Coronavirus, we subscribe to the guidelines set by the local government authorities on firm health and safety regulations. All guests are permitted, except those with comorbidities or are currently pregnant. We request for your understanding in following physical distancing, wearing of face masks and face shields, and other measures to create a safe and low-risk environment for all. During your arrival, you will present the following as required by the Local Government of Calatagan and Laiya, Batangas.

Calatagan South Beach

We can now accommodate guests of ALL AGES from GCQ, MGCQ with Alert Levels 1-3 only.

  1. Please make sure you have a confirmed reservation as walk-ins are still not permitted.
  2. For guests aged 0-11 years old, kindly bring a copy of the birth certificate in replacement for the S-Pass travel permit mentioned on item number 6. The accompanied guardian must have complete requirements.
  3. For guests aged 12-18 years, if not fully vaccinated a negative RT PCR (swab or saliva) test result 72 hours before the time of check-in OR negative Antigen test result 24 hours before check-in, certified by a DOH accredited test center
  4. For guests aged 18 years and above, a vaccination card will suffice only if considered as FULLY VACCINATED (2nd dose was received at least 14 days prior to arrival), otherwise an Antigen or RT PCR Negative Test result shall be required (valid within 72 hours).
  5. Valid Government-issued ID
  6. Please apply for the Travel Coordination permit by registering through S-Pass. 0-11 years don't need to apply for s-pass but must bring a copy of birth certificate. For more information, kindly visit https://s-pass.ph.
Club Laiya

May accommodate guests of ALL AGES from GCQ, MGCQ and with Alert Levels 1-3, except guests from Alert Level 4, and guests with comorbidity or pregnant women. Please see below requirements:

  1. Please make sure you have a confirmed reservation as walk-ins are still not permitted.

  2. We are currently open to all ages. Please see below requirements:

    • For guests 0-2 years old, none as long as the guardians have complete requirements
    • For guests 3-17 years old, a medical certificate from a Physician shall be required. Barangay Health Certificates are not accepted
    • For guests aged 18 years and above, a vaccination card will suffice only if considered as FULLY VACCINATED (2nd dose was received at least 14 days period to arrival), otherwise, an Antigen or RT PCR Negative Test Result shall be required (valid within 72 hours). Batangas residents may only present a Medical Certificate
  3. RT PCR and Antigen test results should be certified by DOH accredited clinics while the Saliva test results must be from Philippine Red Cross.
  4. Ecological Fee Ticket: Please drop by the Municipal Tourism Reception Area at Brgy. Buhay Na Sapa and present your booking confirmation and medical requirements. Pay the Ecological Fee of Php 20.00 per person
  5. Referral Slip: Tourist will claim their Referral Slips at the Municipal Tourism Reception Area if requirements are complete. Resorts are strictly not allowed to accommodate tourists without the Referral Slip

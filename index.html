Club Laiya

May accommodate guests of ALL AGES from GCQ, MGCQ and with Alert Levels 1-3, except guests from Alert Level 4, and guests with comorbidity or pregnant women. Please see below requirements:

  1. Please make sure you have a confirmed reservation as walk-ins are still not permitted.

  2. We are currently open to all ages. Please see below requirements:

    • For guests 0-2 years old, none as long as the guardians have complete requirements.
    • For guests 3-17 years old, a medical certificate from a Physician shall be required. Barangay Health Certificates are not accepted.
    • For guests aged 18 years and above, a vaccination card will suffice only if considered as FULLY VACCINATED (2nd dose was received at least 14 days period to arrival), otherwise, an Antigen or RT PCR Negative Test Result shall be required (valid within 72 hours). Batangas residents may only present a Medical Certificate.
  3. Ecological Fee Ticket: Please drop by the Municipal Tourism Reception Area at Brgy. Buhay Na Sapa and present your booking confirmation and medical requirements. Pay the Ecological Fee of Php 20.00 per person.
  4. Referral Slip: Tourist will claim their Referral Slips at the Municipal Tourism Reception Area if requirements are complete. Resorts are strictly not allowed to accommodate tourists without the Referral Slip.

Calatagan South Beach

We can now accommodate guests of ALL AGES from GCQ, MGCQ and with Alert Levels 1-3, except guests from Alert Level 4.

  1. Please make sure you have a confirmed reservation as walk-ins are still not permitted.
  2. For guests aged below 18 years, a negative RT PCR (swab or saliva) test result 72 hours before the time of check-in OR negative Antigen test result 24 hours before check-in, certified by a DOH accredited test center.
  3. For guests aged 18 years and above, a vaccination card will suffice only if considered as FULLY VACCINATED (2nd dose was received at least 14 days prior to arrival), otherwise an Antigen or RT PCR Negative Test result shall be required (valid within 72 hours).
  4. Valid Government-issued ID
  5. As a precaution when passing check points outside of Calatagan, please apply for the Travel Coordination permit by registering through S-Pass. For more information, kindly visit https://s-pass.ph

Subject to changes depending on latest LGU guidelines.
